The Departments

Cardiology

Our cardiology department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of heart conditions. We provide comprehensive cardiac care, including advanced testing, interventional procedures, and cardiac rehabilitation.

Department Head: Dr. Akshay Shah

Neurology

Our neurology department offers expert care for conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nerves. We have experienced neurologists who provide diagnosis, treatment, and management of various neurological disorders.

Department Head: Dr. Aranya Asnare

Orthopedics

Our orthopedic department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of musculoskeletal conditions, offering advanced surgical and non-surgical solutions for optimal mobility and recovery.

Surgery

Our surgery department is dedicated to providing exceptional surgical care with a team of highly skilled surgeons and advanced technology, ensuring safe procedures and optimal patient outcomes.

Pediatrics

Our Pediatrics department provides comprehensive medical care for infants, children, and adolescents, with a focus on preventive health, early intervention, and compassionate treatment..

Emergency

Our Emergency department operates 24/7, providing immediate medical attention and expert care for critical conditions, accidents, and emergencies with a dedicated team of skilled healthcare professionals.
